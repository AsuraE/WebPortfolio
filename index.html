About Me

Hi, my name's Connor Cooper and I'm studying a Bachelor of Information Technology, majoring in Software Information Systems and Software Design, at the University of Queensland. I'm more of a programmer than a designer, which is reflected in the side projects I work on when I'm not completing university assignments. These usually involve developing emulators, messing around with operating systems for micro computers like the raspberry pi, and designing gaming hardware. I'm set to graduate at the end of this semester.

Technology-wise, I'm proficient in C, C++, Java, Python, SQL, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP. During my time at university I have studided Operating Systems Architecture, Compilers and Interpreters, Computer Networks, Advanced Computer and Network Security, and Advanced Data Structures and Algorithms.
